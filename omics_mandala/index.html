Omics Mandala

Digital Art
2023-5-22

I created this piece as a visual companion to my research paper, published in eBioMedicine in June 2023: Cross-tissue omics analysis discovers ten adipose genes encoding secreted proteins in obesity-related non-alcoholic fatty liver disease.

The piece was featured alongside the article on the eBioMedicine website as one of three featured articles in the June 2023 issue.
